Events Diary

inaugural

08:00 AM Y.S. Chavan Centre, Nariman Point, Mumbai
Sunday 8 September, 2019
 

Bihar Chhatra Sansad and Uttar Bhartiya Yuva Parivartan Manch have come together to celebrate 150th Birth Anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi. On the eve of the day, they are launching an initiative "#Satyagrah2Swaraj" which predominantly focuses on"Ease of Doing Business", "Village Transformation" and "Skilled India". This event is scheduled for 8th Sept 2019 in Mumbai. Bihar Industry Ministry, Ministry of Art, Culture and Youth, Govt. of Bihar, Govt. of Maharashtra, Tata Trust, Bihar foundation and Bravo Pharma (UK) are some of the prestigious names already associated with this event so far.

Our Beliefs

The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams.

  • Concept of the Event

    Ideal occasion to celebrate 150 years of Gandhi. Opportunity to celebrate the unique Gandhian connection between Bihar and Maharashtra. Perpetuate the timeless Gandhian values among Gen-Y. Establish fresh connect with the Bihar and Maharashtra through youth and student program. Revive age old connect with Bihar and Maharashtra. Create opportunities for both states in field of industry/skilling.

  • MISSION

    The mission of Satyagrah2Swaraj is to provide a socially active platform for students of all backgrounds of the Indian society so as to encompass the minds of youth into the spirit of healthy and constructive politics and socially sensitive values for strong nation building by exposing students to all spectrum of ideologies- political, religious, sectarian and thus educating them that ones role in democracy is not just limited to take part in voting process and thereby inspiring the students to be more politically involved, responsive, sensitive and have their unbiased views on political and democratic processes, thereby creating competent public leaders and policy makers willing to work on grassroot to apex levels to make a strong and vibrant India.

  • VISION

    The vision of Satyagrah2Swaraj is to sculpt the most ethical, spirited and capable public leadership in the country, which will shape our policies, make our democracy vibrant and bridge the chasm between the way we are being governed today and the way we actually want to be governed.
